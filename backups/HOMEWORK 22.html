Meet the Owner:

Bailey Mann

My name is Bailey Mann, and I have been visiting and renting properties in Old Orchard ever since I was born. My family and I stayed at a local campground, Wagon Wheel for many summers, and eventually decided to invest in a rental property of our own. I encourage you to visit Old Orchard, for I assure you will not be disapointed!



Here are a few of my favorite places:

  • The Brunswick -
    Restaurant and Bar. Great live bands!
  • The Soda Fountain -
    Best ice cream in the area! With a fun gift shop!
  • Josephs By The Sea -
    Wonderful fine dining and outdoor patio
  • Lisa's Pizza -
    Great pier food and fun downtown vibe!
  • Pier Patio Pub -
    End of pier bar, club atmosphere
  • Beachology -
    Favorite gift shop. Great prices!
